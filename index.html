Lukenya Agrovet Supplies Limited is a Kenyan registered enterprise with over 25 years of experience in the distribution of high-quality farm inputs, Veterinary and Agronomy consultancy services within the Lower Eastern Region of Kenya (Machakos, Makueni, Kitui, and Kajiado Counties).

The enterprise was started in 1998 in Kitengela town as a small Agrovet and was registered as a sole proprietorship the same year, it was incorporated as a private limited company in July 2012. The Company's head office is based in Athi River with branches in Kitengela, Mlolongo, Machakos, and Wote towns.
